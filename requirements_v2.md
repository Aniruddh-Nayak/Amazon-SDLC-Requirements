Functional Requirements:

1.Users should be able to create and manage their accounts.
2.The system shall enable secure online payment processing.
3.Personalized recommendations shall be provided
4.Support for third party sellers shall be provided.
5. Real time order tracking shall be provided.
6.Authorized payment gateways shall be used to enable secure online payment processing through 
7.The system shall generate order confirmation after successful payment.
8.Users should be able to cancel or return orders according to platform policies.
9.The system shall support third-party sellers to register and list products on the platform.
10.Sellers should be able to update product inventory, pricing, and availability.
11.The system shall provide personalized product recommendations based on user activity and purchase history.


Non-Functional Requirements:

1.The system shall support millions of concurrent users.
2.System availability shall exceed 99.9%.
3.Encryption of all financial transactions.
4.Under normal load average response time shall not exceed 2 seconds.
5.The system shall comply with international data protection regulations.
6.During order placement and payment transactions,the system shall ensure data consistency and integrity .
7.The system shall provide fault tolerance to prevent complete system failure in case of partial component outages.
8.To restrict unauthorized access,the platform shall maintain secure authentication and authorization mechanisms. 
9.The system shall provide reliable backup and disaster recovery mechanisms to prevent data loss.
10.The system shall support maintainability by allowing modular updates without affecting unrelated components.
11.The system shall ensure usability through a consistent and user-friendly interface across web and mobile platforms.
